Svar was founded by Ujjwal and Tejas in 2023

with the mission to make speech therapy accessible, affordable and effective for everyone who needs it

How it started ?

Starting this company is quite personal to both Tejas and Ujjwal, sparked by personal experiences.
Tejas' sister suffered from Speech disorder and throughout his childhood he has seen his family struggling to find speech therapy for her. Ujjwal used to stutter as a kid and has firsthand experienced what it does to a child when they cannot express themselves and communicate with others. Every Member of the Team is passionate about Svar's mission and thrives to build a company that makes a change in people's lives.
